For many growing companies, the hardest payroll problem is not calculation—it is timing. Receivables arrive on one calendar; employees must be paid on another.

## Not a consumer loan narrative

Payroll working capital support, as ProQPay frames it, is a structured business enabler. It is intended to help approved clients meet payroll obligations on time, with controls around onboarding, exposure limits, documentation, and settlement.

## When it matters most

- Seasonal revenue concentration
- Project-based billing cycles
- Multi-site growth ahead of cash conversion
- Outsourcing operations with strict payday SLAs

## Guardrails matter

Responsible payroll liquidity support depends on verification, contract structure, monitoring, and clear settlement expectations. The goal is continuity for employees and stability for the employer—not unstructured debt accumulation.

If payday reliability is a strategic risk for your organization, a structured conversation about operating model and funding pathways is worth having early—not during a cut-off emergency.
